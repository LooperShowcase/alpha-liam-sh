What is Pizza?

Pizza is a delicious dish that originated in Italy and has become popular all over the world. It typically consists of a flat, round base of dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients such as meat, vegetables, and herbs.

Types of Pizza

There are many different types of pizza, including:

  • Margherita: Classic pizza with tomato, mozzarella, and basil.
  • Pepperoni: Pizza topped with slices of pepperoni sausage.
  • Vegetarian: Pizza loaded with various fresh vegetables.
  • Hawaiian: Pizza topped with ham and pineapple.

Popular Pizza Toppings

Some of the most popular pizza toppings include:

  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Olives
  • Green peppers
